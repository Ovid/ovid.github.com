# Quickstart: Editor Dropdown Menu

## Usage

1.  **Open Editor**: Navigate to `/editor` or click "Edit" on any article.
2.  **Open Menu**: Click the Hamburger Icon (â˜°) on the right side of the toolbar.
3.  **Actions**:
    *   **Edit Post**: Opens the file selection dialog.
    *   **Upload Image**: Opens the image upload dialog.
4.  **Toggles**:
    *   **Light/Dark Mode**: Click to toggle theme. A checkmark indicates the active state.
    *   **Vim Mode**: Click to toggle Vim keybindings. A checkmark indicates active.
    *   **Syntax Highlight**: Click to toggle syntax highlighting. A checkmark indicates active.
